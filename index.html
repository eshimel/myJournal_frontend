MyJournal

Use MyJournal as a tool to keep a personal account of the what's happening in your life. You can pull up a list of all of your entries, delete previous entries, and rename an entry, if your writing goes in a different direction than when you first started your post. Sorry, no going back in and editing a post. Its a like a paper journal, you'll just have to "rip out the page" by deleting it.
